Why Hibbett · Why Unfazed

Unfazed is the room Hibbett's customer is already in. We want Hibbett in it.

Unfazed Exhibit is Atlanta's most-watched streetwear platform — 21 sold-out editions, 6M+ views per night, 3,000 of the Southeast's most drop-aware Gen Z consumers on the floor. That's not a Hibbett-adjacent audience. That's the Hibbett audience. This is a partnership invitation: Hibbett as the hometown Southeast retailer, Unfazed as the cultural room, both growing the same Atlanta Gen Z together.

01 · SHARED CITY

Hibbett and Unfazed are both built in Atlanta's backyard.

Unfazed is Atlanta-born; Hibbett is Birmingham-HQ’d with the densest Southeast retail footprint in the category. Atlanta is both brands' home turf. A partnership reads like two hometown brands linking — not a corporate sponsorship dropped in from out of town.

02 · SHARED CUSTOMER

The Unfazed floor is the Hibbett customer, dressed up.

Gen Z, Southeast, drop-aware, streetwear-fluent. Hibbett owns the lobby as 3,000 of these consumers walk in — every guest passes through a Hibbett-branded space before they hit the floor. That's a brand impression Hibbett can't manufacture inside its own stores.

03 · SHARED UPSIDE

Hibbett gets content the algorithm rewards.

Unfazed editions clock 6M+ organic views with 86.2% non-follower reach. A Hibbett lobby inside that machine doesn't just get "seen" — it gets discovered by net-new Gen Z consumers Hibbett's paid channels can't reach. Owned distribution, no media buy.

The Hibbett Case · Five reasons this is your moment

This is a Hibbett-shaped opportunity. Not a Hibbett-adjacent one.

01

You own the Southeast Nike consumer.

Distribution · Geography
Hibbett's store footprint is dense across exactly the metros the Unfazed attendee comes from — Atlanta, Birmingham, Charlotte, Memphis, Nashville. Every kid who screenshots a Studio Fleece look from the Aug 29 floor is three exits from a Hibbett. No other Nike retailer can say that about this room.
02

Hometown permission. National retailers can't fake it.

Positioning · Permission
Unfazed is an ATL room. The crowd will read national, mall-agnostic sponsorship as marketing. They will read Hibbett as one of their own — the retailer that grew up in their Southeast malls, that everybody's first pair of Js came from. That permission is the rarest asset in Gen Z sneaker retail and Hibbett already has it. We're proposing you spend it.
03

Content the algorithm rewards — with Hibbett in the frame.

Content · Distribution
Unfazed's 6M+ organic per-edition views aren't a media buy — they're an outcome of the room itself. Putting Hibbett inside that room means every fit pic, every story, every recap reel runs through a Hibbett-branded backdrop. Hibbett walks out with weeks of original content shot by the algorithm's favorite Atlanta room.
04

It signals to Nike where you want to grow.

Partnership · Trade
Nike is publicly resetting around sport, athletes, and community. Hibbett showing up as the retail face of a Nike grassroots culture moment in ATL is a direct signal to Nike's Sports Marketing org that you are the right retail partner for the next allocations, the next exclusives, and the next regional drops.
05

Gen Z female shopper — fully on stage.

Category · Female
5 female designers. 46% female-identifying floor. Nike's category strategy is built around the Gen Z female sneaker + apparel consumer. Hibbett historically over-indexes male; this is the cleanest possible activation to credibly tell a female-customer story without inventing one.

● Campaign concept · The hashtag

#HibbettFitCheck Post your fit from the Hibbett lobby. Win Hibbett. Get reposted by Hibbett. Simple.
